Malinview



About Malinview (2254 McGill St.)

Malinview is a 68-home rental building slated for development in Vancouver's eclectic Grandview-Woodland neighbourhood. Lantern's new development is a stone's throw from Commercial Drive's Little Italy and its organic grocers, restaurants, bars, and specialty shops. Malinview offers convenience, excitement, and rich culture. The development is a short walk from the Skytrain station and Broadway's bus routes. The project is estimated to be under construction by May 2022.
