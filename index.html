About Lantern

Legacy and Commitment

Lantern Properties Ltd. is a multi-generational, multi-family housing operator and developer founded in 1962 by Arthur & Arlene Hayes. The family's first commercial property was a low-rise concrete apartment building in the Ambleside neighbourhood of West Vancouver. Since then, Lantern has steadily grown its portfolio to include properties in several east and west-side Vancouver neighborhoods, as well as the James Bay, Upper Fort, and Rockland areas of Victoria.

In 2015, Lantern initiated and successfully completed a programme to add density to existing sites. Lantern remains committed to providing high-quality housing, managed conscientiously and responsibly, and looks forward to breaking ground on two new multi-family projects in 2019/20. Lantern is actively involved with Landlord BC and the Urban Development Institute, a reflection of Lantern's commitment to the communities in which it operates.

Acknowledgment of Unceded Indigenous Territories

At Lantern Properties we recognize and honor the long-standing history of the land on which our rental properties in Vancouver, BC and Victoria, BC reside. We acknowledge that these lands are situated on the traditional and unceded territories of the Indigenous Peoples, including the Coast Salish, the xʷməθkʷəy̓əm (Musqueam), Skwxwú7mesh (Squamish), and Səl̓ílwətaʔ/Selilwitulh (Tsleil-Waututh) Nations in Vancouver, and the Lekwungen-speaking peoples, specifically the Songhees and Esquimalt Nations, in Victoria.

In the spirit of reconciliation and respect, we express our gratitude for the opportunity to live, work, and thrive on these lands. As a responsible business owner and developer, we are committed to fostering understanding, cooperation, and lasting relationships with the Indigenous communities whose territories we occupy.

We encourage our tenants and visitors to learn about the rich cultural heritage of these Indigenous Nations and to appreciate the resilience and contributions of these communities to the fabric of our society. By acknowledging and honoring their unceded territories, we aim to foster a climate of respect, awareness, and shared responsibility in our communities.

As we move forward together, let us all strive to create a future rooted in collaboration, equity, and understanding, where we cherish and nurture the land and its people for generations to come.

Developments in Progress

Hadera is a collection of 32 rental homes in Victoria's beautiful Rockland community. Hadera is situated approximately 40 feet back from Fort Street, creating a peaceful and quiet environment for residents. Construction commencing Summer 2023.

Kirya is a collection of 11 beautiful rental homes in Vancouver's vibrant West End – a brief walk from English Bay and the abundance of dining and shopping opportunities on Davie Street. Kirya offers residents everything they need in one of the world's most exciting neighbourhoods. The Burrard and the Granville Street bridges are a three-minute drive away, making Kirya an excellent choice for those who wish to make frequent trips out of downtown. Project construction starting September 2021, with completion by December 1, 2023.

Malinview is a 68-home rental building slated for development in Vancouver's eclectic Grandview-Woodland neighbourhood. Lantern's new development is a stone's throw from Commercial Drive's Little Italy and its organic grocers, restaurants, bars, and specialty shops. Malinview offers convenience, excitement, and rich culture. The development is a short walk from the Skytrain station and Broadway's bus routes.

Lantern Properties is pleased to present a rezoning inquiry as a vital component of the Broadway Plan, aimed at expanding the rental stock with 98 brand new rental units. We take pride in our contribution towards the growth of the community by providing affordable housing options. Out of the 140 units, 20% will be available at below-market rates.
